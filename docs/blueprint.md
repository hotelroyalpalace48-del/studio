# **App Name**: Mogra Design Studio

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Browse a curated collection of dress samples and boutique items with images and initial pricing.
- Product Detail Pages: View detailed information, multiple images, and current prices for individual items.
- Admin Product Management: Backend interface for the studio owner to add new dress patterns, upload more images daily, and adjust pricing.
- Shopping Cart: Users can add desired dress samples to a shopping cart before proceeding to purchase.
- Streamlined Checkout: A simplified checkout flow to process orders, apply discounts, and handle payment integration.
- Order Tracking & Returns: Customers can view the status of their orders (delivery) and initiate return requests.
- AI Styling Tool: An AI tool that suggests complementary accessories or design variations for selected dress patterns, enhancing the styling experience.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A rich '4 Leaf Clover' green (#228B22) to symbolize growth, elegance, and natural beauty.
- Background color: Pure white (#FFFFFF) for a clean, spacious, and sophisticated canvas that highlights products.
- Accent color: A lighter, fresh green (#90EE90) used sparingly for calls to action, interactive elements, and subtle highlights.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' (modern serif) for a fashionable and high-end feel, suitable for capturing the elegance of a design studio.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' (humanist sans-serif) for excellent readability and a contemporary touch in product descriptions and general text.
- Minimalist, clean line icons for navigation and actions, ensuring a modern and uncluttered visual experience, aligning with a high-fashion aesthetic.
- Spacious, image-first layouts with ample negative space to emphasize the beauty and detail of each dress pattern, promoting an exclusive boutique feel.
- Subtle and elegant micro-animations on product hovers and smooth transitions between pages to enhance the user experience without distraction.
